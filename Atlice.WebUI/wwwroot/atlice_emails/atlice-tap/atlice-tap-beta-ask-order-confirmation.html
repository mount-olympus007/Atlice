Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I place my Atlice Tap tag?

First have an idea where you want to place it. It can be removed several times depending on the surface where it was placed.
‍
For iPhones: Place your Atlice Tap Tag on the bottom of your phone. If you place it at the top, it will set off your phone's reader constantly!

For most Androids: Place your Atlice Tap Tag on the bottom as well.

For Galaxies/Notes: Place your Atlice Tap Tag at the top of your phone by your camera!

Does the other person need to download an app to share my contact page with them?

Not at all! Just tap with them or have the scan your QR code with their camera and they can connect to any links you have on your contact page.

Your prospect can also download your contact card (vCard) with just a click on the "Save to Contacts" button. Again, there is no need for anyone to download a mobile app,

Does Atlice Tap work with all phones?

Atlice Tap devices use NFC technology and is compatible with almost all mobile phones. Take a look at this list of mobile phones for reference.

After participating in beta testing, can I keep my device?

Yes of course! We appreciate you joining as we provide solutions for leaders, creators and independent professionals who want an easy and safe way to network.
